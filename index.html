Bio

Lorikeet are a rock and roll band from Melbourne. Grounded in 60's rock and psychedelia with share-house garage sensibilities, Lorikeet play dirty songs for troubled dreams and smoke-filled rooms. Lorikeet are not sorry for trashing your kitchen. Lorikeet refuse to leave.
